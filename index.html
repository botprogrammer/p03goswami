FAQs

What technologies do you use?
Being a full stack web developer, I work with both the frontend and the backend.

As a front-end developer, I have worked with HTML/CSS/JS, ReactJs, VueJS, as well as modern libraries like NextJs.

As for the backend, I am comfortable with Python, Django, and Flask, with any database.

Aside from that, I have an excellent knowledge of Figma, Adobe XD, Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, etc.
What if I would like some improvements after the project is completed?
I am available for any changes you wish to make to your project once we have finished working on your project. You can reach me without hesitation if you need some changes made. No matter how minor or how significant your changes are, I will do my best to respond to you right away :)
What is the estimated timeline for completing a project?
Your project completion time will actually depend on how long and complex your project is.

We can even complete it within 5 days if it is just 1-3 pages long! But it may take several days to design and build a complex website, one that integrates databases and payment gateways, has a nice look and feels, and has an attractive interface.

However, I guarantee that no matter when we finish, you will be more than satisfied with the results!
Do you have long-term availability?
It is not appealing for anyone, including myself to change clients on numerous projects. It is for this reason that I prefer to stay in touch even after we have finished a project together. We will not only gain trust in that way, but we will save a great deal of time as well. Ultimately, this is a win-win situation!
